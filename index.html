Deep learning is inspired by the structure of the human brain. Or, ANN (artificial neural network). It uses large sets of data to learn and distinguish the differences between things, like dogs & cats, or in our case, differences in the song features. Power neural networks use those differences to predict songs that you would like to listen to.
